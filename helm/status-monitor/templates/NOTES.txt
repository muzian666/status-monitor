Status Monitor has been deployed!

Get the application URL:
  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app={{ include "status-monitor.name" . }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} $POD_NAME 8000:8000

Then visit: http://localhost:8000

For ingress access, configure your DNS to point status-monitor.local to the ingress IP.
